My Web Development 101 Experience

 

The two main reasons I took this class was to jumpstart my learning of modern programming/scripting languages. As an infrastructure person I used to script in Bash and VBscript. While bash is very good at parsing text and output strings and vbscript/powershell is good at manipulating Microsoft programs They were both lacking in manipulating data from other sources i.e. webpages, ssh session, or third party programs.  The next reason was to meet some good people since I have just moved back to the area after living in Maine for the last decade or so.

Python fit the first reason. It has extensive libraries for several types of programs paramiko for SSH, twill for webpages, and indigo, which is a very popular home automation software that runs on OSX.  And this just scratches the surface.  Also learning some HTML will help me write a simple web interface for some scripting projects I have at work so management can utilize the tools I create.

I also accomplished the second reason for taking the class. I met some cool people I wouldn’t have met if I hacked around at home teaching myself. Everyone was great and I liked working with everyone.

Chad was a good instructor, and very knowledgeable, He was very helpful and always available to answer questions. I enjoyed hearing about his developer “war stories.”

The class was good, I liked that dinner was served. I would come to class straight from work and I did not have time to get something to eat.

The main problem I had with the class is that it was too broad. Maybe split off the Cathedral and the Bazaar into a separate class and spend more time on programming. As interesting as the discussions were leaving that out might give us enough time to create a project. Or have some troubleshooting exercises sort of a “fix this code” assignments. Where chad gave us a script or webpage and we had to figure out why it wasn’t working as intended.  

All in all it was a great experience I would recommend to anyone that needs to get up to speed on newer technologies or is learning them for the first time.
